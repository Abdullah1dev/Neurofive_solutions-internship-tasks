## Summary

All three Large Language Models (ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini) correctly classified all 10 customer support messages using both Zero-Shot and Few-Shot prompting techniques, achieving **100% accuracy** in each experiment.

Although the accuracy remained the same, the Few-Shot prompt provided additional examples that can improve consistency and reduce ambiguity in more complex or domain-specific tasks. In this dataset, the messages were straightforward enough that all three models performed perfectly without requiring example-based guidance.
